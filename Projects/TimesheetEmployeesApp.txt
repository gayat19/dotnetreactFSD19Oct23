Title: Timesheet for Employees HR App

Description:
The Timesheet for Employees HR App is a web-based platform designed to help organizations and Human Resources departments track and manage employee working hours, attendance, and timesheet submissions. It provides an efficient way for employees to record their work hours, and for HR personnel to monitor, review, and manage timesheets, ensuring accurate payroll processing and compliance.

Specifications:

1. User Registration and Authentication:
   - Users, including HR personnel and employees, must register and log in to access the app.
   - Implement secure user authentication using JWT (JSON Web Tokens).

2. Employee Timesheet Submission:
   - Allow employees to submit daily, weekly, or monthly timesheets, recording total hours worked.
   - Enable employees to add comments or notes to their timesheet entries.

3. Approval Workflow:
   - Implement an approval process for timesheet submissions, including review and approval by supervisors or HR personnel.
   - Send email or in-app notifications to users when actions are required (e.g., approval, rejection, revision).

4. Overtime Tracking:
   - Automatically calculate and track overtime hours based on predefined rules and company policies.
   - Ensure accurate overtime pay calculations.

5. Leave and Absence Management:
   - Allow employees to request and manage time-off and leave requests within the app.
   - Provide HR personnel with tools to review and approve leave requests.

6. Attendance Monitoring(Optional):
   - Enable HR personnel to monitor and track employee attendance
   - Generate attendance reports for management.

7. Project and Task Tracking:
   - Allow employees to associate timesheet entries with specific projects, clients, or tasks.
   - Provide insights into the allocation of working hours to different projects.

8. Reports and Analytics:
   - Offer HR professionals tools for generating reports and analytics on employee working hours, attendance, and productivity.
   - Provide insights into time management and workforce efficiency.

9. User Profiles:
   - Users can maintain profiles with personal information, contact details, and job titles.
   - Allow users to manage and edit their profiles.

10. Data Security:
    - Ensure the security and privacy of user data, timesheet records, and sensitive payroll information.
    - Implement data encryption and access controls.

11. Responsive Design:
    - Ensure the app is responsive, making it accessible on various devices and screen sizes.

12. Database:
    - Use a database system (e.g., PostgreSQL or MySQL) for storing user profiles, timesheet data, and attendance records.

13. API Integration:
    - Integrate with payroll and HR systems to facilitate seamless payroll processing and reporting.

14. Testing and Debugging:
    - Perform thorough testing and debugging to ensure the app functions without errors.

15. Deployment:
    - Deploy the app to a web server or cloud platform for internal access by HR personnel and employees.

The Timesheet for Employees HR App is designed to improve the accuracy of time tracking, attendance monitoring, and leave management within an organization. It aims to streamline HR operations and ensure compliance with labor laws and company policies.
