Title: Stock Management for Store with Billing App

Description:
The Stock Management for Store with Billing App is a comprehensive solution designed for businesses and retail stores to efficiently manage their inventory, track stock levels, generate bills, and facilitate seamless point-of-sale transactions. This app provides a user-friendly interface for inventory control, billing, and sales management, ultimately improving operational efficiency.

Specifications:

1. User Registration and Authentication:
   - Users, including store managers, cashiers, and staff, must register and log in to access the app.
   - Implement secure user authentication using credentials.

2. Inventory Management:
   - Allow users to create, edit, and delete product listings, including details such as product name, description, price, and stock levels.
   - Implement support for categories and barcodes for easy product identification.

3. Stock Tracking:
   - Enable real-time stock tracking to monitor product availability.
   - Provide notifications or alerts when stock levels fall below a predefined threshold.

4. Point-of-Sale (POS):
   - Offer a user-friendly POS system for cashiers to select products, calculate totals, apply discounts, and generate bills for customers.
   - Support multiple payment methods (cash, card, mobile payments).

5. Bill Generation:
   - Generate itemized bills with details of products, quantities, prices, and taxes.
   - Include invoice numbers and timestamps for tracking purposes.

6. Receipt Printing:
   - Integrate with receipt printers for the generation of printed receipts for customers.
   - Provide options for electronic receipts via email or SMS.

7. Sales Reports(Optional):
   - Offer tools for generating sales reports, including daily, weekly, and monthly summaries.
   - Enable export of sales data in various formats (e.g., CSV, PDF).

8. Return and Refund Handling:
   - Implement a system for processing returns and refunds with appropriate validation and approval mechanisms.

9. User Roles and Permissions:
   - Define user roles (e.g., store manager, cashier) with varying levels of access and permissions.
   - Ensure data privacy and access controls.

10. Inventory Search:
    - Allow users to search for products by name, category, or barcode for quick access and retrieval.

11. Supplier Integration:
    - Integrate with suppliers' systems for automated restocking and purchase order generation.

12. Security:
    - Implement security measures to protect transaction data, user credentials, and sensitive information.


13. Testing and Debugging:
    - Perform thorough testing to ensure the app functions without errors.

14. Deployment:
    - Deploy the app on devices suitable for point-of-sale use, such as tablets or dedicated POS terminals.

The Stock Management for Store with Billing App is designed to streamline inventory management and sales processes, enhance customer service, and improve overall operational efficiency for retail businesses and stores. It offers a user-friendly platform for stock control, billing, and sales management.
